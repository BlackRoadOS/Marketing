17 products share one memory system. No copy-paste between apps. No exporting and importing. Everything rides on one highway.

## The Math

BlackRoad OS is built on the Amundson Framework — a mathematical system for coherence that inverts the Boltzmann factor:

```
K(t) = C(t) * e^(lambda * |delta|)
```

Systems that follow Boltzmann decay toward cold equilibrium. This formula says: coherence amplifies under contradiction. When a new device joins, when a new person arrives, when something unexpected happens — that's delta. And the system grows from it.
