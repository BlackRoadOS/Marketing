## Visual Direction

### Option A — The Desktop Shot
Screenshot of os.blackroad.io with multiple draggable windows open (RoadCode, RoadTrip chat, Roadie tutor). Dark background, gradient accent bar at top. Clean, real — not a mockup. Show the actual product.

### Option B — The Highway
Dark, cinematic wide shot. A glowing road stretching forward, lit with the brand gradient (Ember to Cyan: #FF6B2B through #00D4FF). Small icons or labels for each product appear along the road like highway signs. Minimal text overlay: "17 products. One road. Yours."

### Option C — Carousel (5 slides)
1. "What if your entire digital life fit in one tab?" (dark bg, white text)
2. Screenshot of the OS desktop with windows open
3. Product grid — 17 icons with names, clean layout on black
4. "Every action verified. Every mile rewarded." (gradient text)
5. "os.blackroad.io — Remember the Road. Pave Tomorrow." (CTA slide)

### Recommended: Option C (Carousel)
Carousels get 3x the engagement on Instagram. Five slides tells the story without cramming.

## Post Notes

- No emojis in caption (brand rule)
- Brand gradient for any graphic elements, never for text
- Text in images: white or light gray only
- Aspect: 1:1 for feed, 9:16 for stories/reels version
