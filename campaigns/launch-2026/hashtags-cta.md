1. Reply to every comment within 2 hours for the first 48 hours
2. Never use emojis in posts or replies (brand rule — warmth comes from words)
3. Always link to os.blackroad.io, not individual product URLs (drive to the OS first)
4. Share numbers when asked: 17 products, 5 Pis, 52 TOPS AI acceleration, 1 founder, 1 year
5. When someone asks "is this real?" — screenshot the live desktop and reply with it
