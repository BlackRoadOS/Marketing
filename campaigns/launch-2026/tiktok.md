## Video 1 — "I Built an OS on Raspberry Pis" (45-60 sec)

### Hook (first 3 seconds)
[Camera: close-up of Raspberry Pi cluster with LED lights blinking]
"I spent a year building an entire operating system on these."

### Body
[Cut to: screen recording of os.blackroad.io loading — full desktop appears in browser]
"One browser tab. That's it. You get a full desktop with 17 apps that all talk to each other."

[Cut to: opening RoadTrip chat, agents responding]
"These are AI agents. They remember every conversation you've ever had with them. Not just this session — forever."

[Cut to: opening RoadCode, code appearing]
"Code editor with Claude built in. Social media manager that posts while you sleep. A tutor that picks up exactly where you left off last month."

[Cut to: RoadChain showing verified blocks]
"Every single action gets verified on a blockchain. And you earn tokens for using the platform."

### Closer
[Cut to: Alexa at desk with Pis visible behind her]
"I built this because I got tired of renting my entire digital life from companies that don't know my name. Five Raspberry Pis. One year. Zero big tech."

[Text overlay: os.blackroad.io]
"Remember the Road. Pave Tomorrow."

### Visual Style
- Dark room, monitor glow, Pi LED ambiance
- Screen recordings are real (not mockups)
- Quick cuts (2-3 sec each) to maintain energy
- Text overlays in white, minimal, lower third
- Brand gradient bar as a subtle persistent element (top or bottom of frame)
- Background music: lo-fi electronic, building energy

## Post Notes

- Vertical 9:16 format
- Captions/subtitles always on (80% of TikTok is watched on mute)
- Post at 6-10 PM EST for highest engagement
- No emojis in on-screen text (brand rule)
- Use trending sounds where they fit, but prioritize original audio for the founder-story videos
- Cross-post to Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts with same content
