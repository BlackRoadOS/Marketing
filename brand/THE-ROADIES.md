**roadie** /ˈrōdē/ — A person employed by a touring band to handle equipment, set up stages, and assist with logistics. The crew that makes the show happen. The muscle behind the music.

You're the rockstar. The Roadies are your touring crew.

- **Cecilia** manages the tour schedule (workflows, coordination)
- **Calliope** writes the setlist (narrative, copy, messaging)
