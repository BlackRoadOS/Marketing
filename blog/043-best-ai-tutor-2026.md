No other AI tutor offers this range:

- **Check** — Verify work, identify gaps.
- **Solve** — Full solution with explanation (only after genuine attempt).
- **ELI5** — Simplify for younger students.

Students switch freely between modes. The AI adapts difficulty in real-time based on performance — harder when they're cruising, easier when they're stuck.
